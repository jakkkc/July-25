Opportunities & Wins

Opportunities

  • TikTok Dominance: Capitalize on viral momentum with exclusive offers and UGC campaigns
  • Staff Engagement Program: Create "Content Ambassador" program with recognition incentives
  • Conversion Optimization: Improve Facebook CTAs and implement retargeting campaigns
  • Cross-Platform Synergy: Develop integrated campaigns leveraging Instagram link clicks and TikTok views

Major Wins

  • Chams Media Collaboration: Professional photography and social media exposure by Chams Media, which has a massive audience, for both properties
  • TikTok Breakthrough: 253% follower growth (HPC) and 99,999% metric growth (HPT)
  • Management Transition: Successfully adapted strategy under new leadership with no performance drop
  • Cross-Platform Growth: Achieved triple-digit growth across Facebook, Instagram and TikTok
  • Content Strategy: Developed viral-worthy content driving 1,100% Facebook page visit increase

Recommendations

1. Formalize TikTok success into a repeatable content playbook
2. Implement staff content quotas with monthly recognition program
3. Allocate budget for TikTok-specific promotions and influencer collaborations
4. Develop cross-departmental content workshops to improve staff participation
5. Test shoppable features on Instagram to capitalize on link click growth
